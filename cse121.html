About Us

Building Character Since 1995

Shantiniketan School has been a pillar of quality education for nearly three decades. Our CBSE-affiliated institution blends rigorous academics with arts, sports, and social values to shape well-rounded individuals.

    Academic RigourFrom Grade 1 to 12, our curriculum challenges and inspires curiosity in every subject.
